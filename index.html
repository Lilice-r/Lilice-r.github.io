Biography

I am currently a Tenure-track Associate Professor (预聘副研究员) at Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Before that, I received the Ph.D. degree from the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Yanan Cao and Prof. Wei Ma. My research interests include Natural Language Processing and AI Security.
